Task One – User Interview and Requirements Gathering
Introduction
Our team interviewed several target users to understand their expectations and requirements for the Food Delivery Website project. 
The purpose of the interview was to identify common problems users face when ordering food online and determine useful features 
for our software.
The interviews were conducted with university students and working adults who frequently use online food delivery services.

Interview Questions
-How often do you order food online?
-What problems do you usually face with food delivery apps?
-What features do you think are most important?
-What payment methods do you prefer?
-Would you like order tracking functionality?
-What makes you choose one food delivery app over another?

Interview Summary
User 1	Wants fast checkout and simple UI
User 2	Needs real-time order tracking
User 3	Prefers multiple payment methods
User 4	Wants restaurant ratings and reviews
User 5	Needs mobile-friendly design

Identified Requirements
Based on the interviews, the team identified the following requirements:
    Functional requirements
        -User registration and login
        -Browse food menu
        -Search food items
        -Add items to cart
        -Online payment
        -Order tracking
        -Restaurant ratings and reviews
        -Admin management system

    Non-Functional Requirements
        -Responsive design
        -Fast loading speed
        -Secure payment system
        -Easy-to-use interface

Conclusion
The interviews helped the team better understand user expectations for a food delivery platform. Based on the feedback, 
we created a list of user stories with priorities and estimated development effort. 
These user stories will guide the development process for future iterations of the project.
